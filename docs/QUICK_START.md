## First Time Setup (5 minutes)

### Step 1: Set Up Your Accounts
1. Click the **"Accts"** button (top row)
2. For each bank account:
   - Type the account name (like "Savings Account")
   - Type a short label for the button (like "Saving")
   - Click "Add Account"
3. You'll see a new button appear for each account

### Step 2: Create Your Budget
1. Click the **"Bdgt"** button (top row)
2. Add your spending categories:
   - Type category name (like "Groceries")
   - Type monthly amount (like "400")
   - Click "Add Budget"
3. Do this for all your regular expenses:
   - Groceries
   - Gas
   - Utilities
   - Entertainment
   - Eating Out
   - Shopping
   - etc.

### Step 3: You're Ready! üéâ
Now you can add transactions and track your spending!

## Daily Use

### Adding a Transaction
1. Make sure the right account button is highlighted (purple)
2. Click **"Add"**
3. Fill in:
   - Date
   - What it was for (Description)
   - Category (pick from your budget list)
   - Amount
   - Type (Withdrawal or Deposit)
4. Click "Save Transaction"

### Checking Your Budget
- Click **"Bdgt"** anytime
- See how much you've spent in each category
- Red numbers mean you're over budget (time to slow down!)
- Budget resets automatically each month

### Switching Accounts
- Just click the account button (top row)
- That account's transactions will show
- Each account has its own balance

## Monthly Routine

### Reconciling Your Bank Statement

**What is reconciling?**
It's matching your register with your bank statement to make sure everything is correct.

**Easy Way (Recommended):**
1. Download your bank statement as CSV file
2. Click **"CSV"** button
3. Choose **"Auto Reconciliation"**
4. Click "Import CSV File"
5. Select your downloaded file
6. Review what it found
7. Click "Confirm Import"

‚ú® The app will:
- Match transactions automatically
- Mark matched ones as reconciled (‚úì)
- Add any you missed
- Tell you if amounts don't match

**What if something doesn't match?**
- Look for the transaction in your register
- Check if you entered it differently
- Make sure the amounts match
- Correct any mistakes

## Common Questions

**Q: How do I know which account I'm looking at?**
A: The button is highlighted in purple with a dark border.

**Q: What if I accidentally added a transaction to the wrong account?**
A: Delete it (click the red X), switch accounts, then add it again.

**Q: Can I undo a delete?**
A: No - that's why it asks "Are you sure?" Always double-check!

**Q: Why is my budget showing I spent more than I allowed?**
A: You went over budget in that category. Time to cut back!

**Q: What if my bank's CSV file doesn't work?**
A: Try adding transactions manually, or ask for help - we may need to add support for your bank.

**Q: Where is my data stored?**
A: On your phone/computer only. It never goes to the internet.

**Q: What if I get a new phone?**
A: Click "Save" to download your data, then "Load" on new phone.

## Getting Help

**Something not working right?**
1. Try closing and reopening the app
2. Check that you're in the right account
3. Make sure you clicked "Save Transaction"
4. Look at the ENHANCED_README file for detailed help

**Lost your data?**
- Check if you have a backup JSON file (from "Save" button)
- Check if transactions are just in a different account
- The app auto-saves to your device constantly
